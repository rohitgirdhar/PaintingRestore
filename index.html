    Painting Restore

    PaintingRestore is a mobile (android) application that acts as a restoring lens for paintings in exhibitions. Simply point the camera to the distorted painting of interest, and the restored painting (as it looked originally) will be overlay-ed over the image, exactly in the angle/orientation you view it from!
